Beaches

PAGE 2

Canibad Beach – Samal Island, Davao del Norte

A hidden paradise in Samal, Canibad Beach is known for its secluded location, towering cliffs, and rustic charm, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Dahican Beach – Mati, Davao Oriental

A paradise for surfers and skimboarders, Dahican Beach is a scenic 7-kilometer stretch of golden sand and powerful waves.

Dakak Beach- Zamboanga del Norte

A pristine white-sand beach with crystal-clear waters and lush coconut trees, Dakak Beach is a tropical paradise known for its luxurious resort and thrilling water activities.

Dumaluan Beach – Bohol

A serene stretch of white sand in Bohol, known for its gentle waves, family-friendly resorts, and laid-back atmosphere. Ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and water activities, it's a great alternative to the more crowded Alona Beach.

El Nido – Palawan

A tropical paradise in Palawan, renowned for its dramatic limestone formations, hidden lagoons, and pristine beaches. Island-hopping tours reveal breathtaking seascapes, while its vibrant marine life makes it a top destination for diving and snorkeling. El Nido is a breathtaking tropical paradise located at the northern tip of Palawan.
