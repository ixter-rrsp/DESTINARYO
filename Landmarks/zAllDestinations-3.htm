Philippines Tourist Spots

PAGE 3

Canibad Beach – Samal Island, Davao del Norte

A hidden paradise in Samal, Canibad Beach is known for its secluded location, towering cliffs, and rustic charm, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Casa Manila – Manila

A Spanish colonial-style museum in Manila that showcases period furniture, artifacts, and architecture from the 19th century. It provides visitors with an immersive experience of the lavish lifestyle of wealthy Filipino families during Spanish rule.

Casaroro Falls – Valencia, Negros Oriental

Hidden in Valencia, Negros Oriental, this towering waterfall requires a challenging trek down steep steps. It rewards visitors with a mesmerizing, narrow cascade that plunges into a cold, deep pool, ideal for adventurous souls seeking a nature escape.

Cebu Ocean Park – Cebu

Cebu Ocean Park is the largest oceanarium in the Philippines, offering a diverse marine life experience with massive aquariums, interactive exhibits, and animal encounters. Located in Cebu City, this world-class attraction showcases local and exotic marine species and provides an educational and entertaining experience for visitors of all ages.

Clark Aqua Planet – Pampanga

Clark Aqua Planet is one of the largest water parks in Southeast Asia, covering 10 hectares of thrilling water attractions. Located inside Clark Freeport Zone in Pampanga, this state-of-the-art water park features 38 water slides and attractions, making it a premier destination for families, thrill-seekers, and water lovers.

Clark Safari and Adventure Park – Pampanga

A wildlife and recreational destination featuring over 1,500 animals, thrilling adventure rides, and interactive exhibits. Visitors can enjoy safari tours, animal encounters, and educational experiences in a nature-friendly environment.

Dahican Beach – Mati, Davao Oriental

A paradise for surfers and skimboarders, Dahican Beach is a scenic 7-kilometer stretch of golden sand and powerful waves.

Dahilayan AdventurePark – Bukidnon

A top extreme adventure park in Bukidnon, offering Asia’s longest dual zipline, rope courses, and ATV rides. Its cool mountain setting and adrenaline-pumping activities make it a must-visit for thrill-seekers and nature lovers.

Dakak Beach- Zamboanga del Norte

A pristine white-sand beach with crystal-clear waters and lush coconut trees, Dakak Beach is a tropical paradise known for its luxurious resort and thrilling water activities.

Danao Adventure Park – Bohol

Located in Bohol, this eco-adventure park features heart-pounding activities such as canyon swinging, ziplining, caving, and rappelling. Nestled in a lush landscape, it provides a thrilling outdoor experience for adventure enthusiasts.
