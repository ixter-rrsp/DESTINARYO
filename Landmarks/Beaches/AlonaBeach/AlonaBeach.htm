Alona Beach

Panglao, Bohol

Alona Beach is a tropical paradise known for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear waters, and lively coastal charm. Stretching about 1.5 kilometers along the southwestern tip of Panglao Island, this stunning beach is a haven for sun-seekers, divers, and island explorers. The shoreline is lined with cozy resorts, beachfront restaurants, and lively bars, offering the perfect mix of relaxation and excitement. Whether you’re lounging under swaying palm trees, snorkeling in vibrant coral reefs, or savoring fresh seafood as the sun sets, Alona Beach captures the magic of island living. The beach’s gentle waves and shallow waters make it ideal for swimming, while nearby dive spots, like Balicasag Island, attract divers from all over the world.

Beyond its natural beauty, Alona Beach is the beating heart of Panglao’s tourism scene, buzzing with activity day and night. Mornings are perfect for island-hopping adventures and dolphin-watching tours, while evenings come alive with live music, fire dancers, and beachfront cocktails. The laid-back island atmosphere is balanced by the convenience of modern amenities, from luxury resorts to quaint guesthouses, making it an accessible escape for all kinds of travelers. Whether you’re seeking thrilling water activities, a romantic beach getaway, or simply a spot to unwind and watch the waves, Alona Beach delivers an unforgettable experience in one of Bohol’s most picturesque destinations.

Alona Beach

Alona Beach offers a little bit of everything — pristine nature, vibrant nightlife, and easy access to some of Bohol’s top attractions. It’s a gateway to world-class diving, with nearby sites teeming with marine life, sea turtles, and stunning coral gardens. The beach itself is perfect for leisurely strolls, with plenty of seaside cafés and shops to explore. As the sky turns shades of pink and orange at sunset, the beach transforms into a lively hub where travelers and locals gather to celebrate island life. Whether you spend your days exploring hidden coves, hopping on a paddleboard, or simply soaking up the sun, Alona Beach offers a dreamy escape that blends relaxation and adventure in equal measure.
