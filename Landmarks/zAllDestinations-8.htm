Philippines Tourist Spots

PAGE 8

Mount Pinatubo – Zambales, Tarlac & Pampanga

Famous for its cataclysmic 1991 eruption, this Zambales volcano now features a stunning crater lake. Trekking through its ash-laden landscapes offers a unique experience, with breathtaking views of the turquoise waters nestled within its rugged caldera.

Mount Pulag – Camiguin

The highest peak in Luzon, Mt. Pulag is famous for its sea of clouds, golden grasslands, and stunning sunrise views that make it a bucket-list destination for hikers.

Nagtabon Beach – Puerto Princesa, Palawan

A hidden gem in Puerto Princesa, Palawan, offering golden sand, rolling waves, and a peaceful, undeveloped coastline. It's an ideal spot for surfing, beachcombing, or simply enjoying the sunset in a serene, crowd-free environment.

National Museum – Manila

A treasure trove of Philippine history, art, and culture, the National Museum of the Philippines houses centuries-old artifacts, masterpieces, and relics that tell the country’s rich story.

Ocean Adventure – Subic

A marine park in Subic, featuring live dolphin and sea lion shows, interactive exhibits, and snorkeling experiences. It provides an educational and entertaining experience while promoting marine conservation and wildlife protection.

Pagsanjan Falls – Laguna

One of the most famous waterfalls in the Philippines, Pagsanjan Falls is a stunning three-tiered cascade best known for its thrilling canoe ride adventure through lush gorges and rapid streams.

Paliton Beach – Siquijor

Often called the "Little Boracay of Siquijor," this beach features fine white sand, swaying palm trees, and crystal-clear waters. It’s a perfect spot for watching breathtaking sunsets and enjoying a peaceful tropical getaway on the mystical island.

Paoay Church – Ilocos Norte

A UNESCO-listed baroque church in Ilocos Norte, known for its massive buttresses and earthquake-resistant design. Built in the 18th century, it remains an architectural marvel and an important religious and historical landmark in the region.

Paradise Beach – Bantayan Island, Cebu

A serene, white-sand beach with sparkling blue waters and a laid-back vibe, Kaputian Beach is a perfect escape for those seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

Patar Beach - Pangasinan

A hidden gem in Pangasinan, known for its golden sand, clear blue waters, and breathtaking sunsets. Its unspoiled beauty makes it a perfect spot for swimming, beach camping, and relaxing by the scenic Lingayen Gulf.
