Philippines Tourist Spots

PAGE 6

Lake Holon – South Cotabato

A hidden gem in Mindanao, Lake Holon is a pristine crater lake surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills, offering a peaceful retreat for nature lovers.

Lake Sebu – South Cotabato

A tranquil highland lake known for its cultural significance and stunning landscapes, Lake Sebu is home to the T’boli tribe and is one of the top eco-tourism destinations in the Philippines.

Long Beach - San Vicente, Palawan

The longest white-sand beach in the Philippines, Long Beach in San Vicente is a tranquil paradise offering an unspoiled escape with breathtaking coastal views.

Lugnason Falls – Siquijor

A serene waterfall in Siquijor, offering a peaceful escape with its clear blue waters and lush surroundings. Known for its zodiac-themed pool, it's a hidden gem for those seeking a quiet, nature-filled retreat on the mystical island.

Mactan Shrine – Cebu

Mactan Shrine in Cebu commemorates the historic Battle of Mactan, where Lapu-Lapu defeated Ferdinand Magellan in 1521. Surrounded by scenic coastal views, it features monuments honoring the bravery and heroism of the island’s warriors.

Magik Land – Negros Occidental

The first outdoor theme park in Negros Occidental, featuring thrilling rides, carnival attractions, and fantasy-themed entertainment. It is designed for fun and adventure, making it a great destination for families and theme park enthusiasts.

Magpupungko Rock Pools – Surigao del Norte

A serene stretch of white sand in Bohol, known for its gentle waves, family-friendly resorts, and laid-back atmosphere. Ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and water activities, it's a great alternative to the more crowded Alona Beach.

Manila Ocean Park – Manila

A world-class marine theme park in Manila, offering interactive exhibits, ocean tunnels, and fascinating marine shows. Visitors can explore diverse aquatic species, experience fish spa treatments, and even swim with stingrays and sharks.

Maria Cristina Falls – Iligan City, Lanao del Norte

Known as the “Twin Falls” of the Philippines, Maria Cristina Falls is a spectacular 98-meter-high waterfall that serves as a major source of hydroelectric power in Mindanao.

Mayon Volcano – Albay

One of the world's most perfectly shaped volcanoes, Mayon in Albay is both stunning and active. Its picturesque cone dominates the landscape, offering adventure activities like trekking, ATV rides, and a breathtaking view from nearby ruins and farms.
