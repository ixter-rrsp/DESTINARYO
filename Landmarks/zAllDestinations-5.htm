Philippines Tourist Spots

PAGE 5

Glorious Fantasyland – Zamboanga del Norte

A grand amusement park in Zamboanga del Norte, featuring colorful rides, dazzling light shows, and live entertainment. It is one of Mindanao’s biggest theme parks, providing fun-filled experiences for visitors of all ages.

Hinulugang Taktak – Antipolo, Rizal

A historic waterfall in Antipolo, Rizal, once a sacred bathing place. Now a protected park, it features a wide cascade, picnic spots, and a tranquil ambiance, making it a great getaway near Metro Manila for relaxation.

Inflatable Island – Subic Bay, Zambales

Asia’s largest floating playground, featuring giant slides, swings, and obstacle courses on water. This vibrant attraction offers exciting activities, perfect for thrill-seekers and families looking for a fun beach adventure.

J Castles ThemePark – Laguna

A fairy tale-inspired attraction in Laguna, featuring castle-like structures, themed rides, and magical landscapes. It offers a whimsical experience for families and kids who dream of stepping into a storybook adventure.

Jose Rizal Shrine – Calamba

The Jose Rizal Shrine in Calamba, Laguna, is the reconstructed ancestral home of Dr. José Rizal, the Philippines’ national hero. This Spanish-colonial-style house is where Rizal was born and raised, making it a significant landmark in Philippine history.

Kalanggaman Island – Leyte

A picturesque sandbar island in Leyte, famous for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil surroundings. It's a perfect destination for beach camping, snorkeling, and enjoying a pristine tropical escape away from commercialization.

Kaparkan Falls – Abra

One of the most unique waterfalls in the Philippines, Kaparkan Falls in Abra is known for its terraced limestone formations, creating a breathtaking staircase of cascading water.

Kaputian Beach – Samal, Davao del Norte

A serene, white-sand beach with sparkling blue waters and a laid-back vibe, Kaputian Beach is a perfect escape for those seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

Kawasan Falls – Cebu

A world-renowned multi-tiered waterfall known for its stunning blue waters, Kawasan Falls is a paradise for adventure seekers and relaxation lovers alike.

Lake Balinsasayao – Negros Oriental

A twin crater lake in Negros Oriental, surrounded by thick forests and rich biodiversity. Its calm waters are perfect for kayaking, while the surrounding trails offer panoramic views and encounters with rare bird species in a tranquil setting.
