Lakes and Mountains

PAGE 1

Bud Bongao – Tawi-Tawi

The sacred mountain of Tawi-Tawi, Bud Bongao is a pilgrimage site and eco-tourism destination, known for its stunning island views and friendly long-tailed macaques.

Caliraya Lake – Laguna

A man-made lake in Laguna, ideal for fishing, kayaking, and water sports. Surrounded by rolling hills and cool breezes, it offers a peaceful retreat, scenic boat rides, and cozy lakeside resorts perfect for weekend getaways.

Lake Balinsasayao – Negros Oriental

A twin crater lake in Negros Oriental, surrounded by thick forests and rich biodiversity. Its calm waters are perfect for kayaking, while the surrounding trails offer panoramic views and encounters with rare bird species in a tranquil setting.

Lake Holon – South Cotabato

A hidden gem in Mindanao, Lake Holon is a pristine crater lake surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills, offering a peaceful retreat for nature lovers.

Lake Sebu – South Cotabato

A tranquil highland lake known for its cultural significance and stunning landscapes, Lake Sebu is home to the T’boli tribe and is one of the top eco-tourism destinations in the Philippines.
