Taytay Falls

Laguna

Taytay Falls, also known as Imelda Falls, is a serene paradise tucked within the lush forests of Majayjay, Laguna. The waterfall flows from the slopes of Mount Banahaw, cascading from a rocky cliff into an ice-cold, crystal-clear basin below. The water is refreshingly chilly, perfect for cooling off after a short trek through the verdant forest. The trail to the falls is relatively easy, with well-maintained paths surrounded by dense foliage, making the journey as scenic as the destination. The falls themselves may not be towering, but their beauty lies in their simplicity — a gentle, uninterrupted flow of water surrounded by moss-covered rocks and vibrant greenery. The peaceful atmosphere, combined with the sound of flowing water and birdsong, makes Taytay Falls a tranquil haven for nature lovers.

The area around the falls is perfect for day trips and overnight camping, with plenty of flat spaces to pitch tents and enjoy nature up close. Visitors often spend hours swimming in the cool waters, lounging on the rocks, or exploring the nearby streams. The site is well-loved by locals and tourists alike, yet it retains a quiet, unspoiled charm. Small huts and picnic areas are available, but the site remains blissfully free of heavy commercial development. The best time to visit is during the dry season when the water is clearer, though the falls flow year-round thanks to the mountain springs. Whether you’re seeking a quick escape from city life or a peaceful weekend getaway, Taytay Falls offers a refreshing retreat into Laguna’s natural wonders.

Taytay Falls

Taytay Falls is a hidden gem that captures the essence of Laguna’s lush landscapes. The short trek to the falls is an enjoyable mini-adventure, filled with tropical flora and the occasional sighting of local wildlife. Upon arrival, visitors are greeted by the falls' calming presence, with the icy water inviting swimmers to take a plunge. The camping-friendly environment allows guests to fully immerse themselves in nature, falling asleep to the soothing sound of rushing water. Local caretakers keep the area clean, and visitors are encouraged to practice "Leave No Trace" principles to preserve the falls’ pristine beauty. Despite its growing popularity, Taytay Falls remains a peaceful sanctuary, perfect for those seeking a simple, nature-filled getaway. It’s a place where time slows down, letting you relax, recharge, and reconnect with nature’s purest elements.
