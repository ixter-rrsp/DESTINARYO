Aliwagwag Falls

Davao Oriental

Aliwagwag Falls, often hailed as the crown jewel of Davao Oriental, is a majestic series of cascading waterfalls that stretch over 84 tiers, making it the tallest waterfall system in the Philippines. Surrounded by lush rainforests, the falls resemble a colossal stairway, with water gracefully tumbling from one level to the next. The powerful rush of water creates a soothing symphony, while the mist that rises from the cascades adds a mystical charm to the landscape. Visitors can marvel at the grandeur of the falls from various vantage points or take a dip in the cool, crystal-clear pools below. The site’s unspoiled beauty and sheer scale make Aliwagwag Falls a paradise for nature lovers and photographers alike.

The area around Aliwagwag Falls is rich with biodiversity, offering a glimpse of Mindanao’s vibrant flora and fauna. Adventurous souls can explore nearby trails, cross hanging bridges, or relax in designated picnic areas while soaking in the scenery. The falls are part of the Aliwagwag Protected Landscape, ensuring the preservation of its pristine environment and wildlife. For those seeking an immersive experience, the surrounding forests provide an excellent backdrop for trekking and birdwatching. Whether you’re craving relaxation or adventure, Aliwagwag Falls promises an unforgettable encounter with nature’s raw beauty. It’s a destination that captures the heart and spirit of the Philippines’ wild landscapes.

Aliwagwag Falls

Aliwagwag Falls is more than just a stunning sight — it's a testament to the untamed beauty of Davao Oriental. The falls' tiered formation makes each level feel like a distinct wonder, with hidden alcoves and serene pools waiting to be explored. Visitors can swim in the refreshing waters, feel the force of the cascades up close, or simply relax on the smooth rocks while listening to the roar of the falls. Local guides are available to share stories about the area's history and cultural significance, adding depth to the visit. The nearby eco-park offers amenities for guests, including cottages and viewing decks for a more comfortable experience. Despite its grandeur, Aliwagwag Falls retains a sense of tranquility, making it an ideal spot for reflection and connection with nature. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or a peace-seeker, this waterfall is a treasure that leaves a lasting impression.
