Casaroro Falls

Valencia, Negros Oriental

Hidden within the lush forests of Valencia, Negros Oriental, Casaroro Falls is a majestic natural wonder renowned for its towering cascade and tranquil surroundings. The waterfall plunges from a height of around 100 feet, creating a powerful, narrow stream that crashes into a deep, refreshing basin below. Enclosed by steep cliffs and dense vegetation, the falls offer an intimate and secluded atmosphere, perfect for nature lovers seeking a serene escape. The sound of the rushing water, echoing through the canyon, adds to the dramatic allure of the place. While reaching Casaroro Falls requires a descent down a steep staircase and a trek across rocky terrain, the reward is a breathtaking view of one of the most beautiful waterfalls in the region.

The journey to Casaroro Falls is an adventure in itself, immersing visitors in Negros Oriental’s raw, untouched landscapes. The trek involves crossing streams, navigating boulders, and following forest trails, making the experience even more rewarding for hikers and thrill-seekers. Once at the base, visitors can cool off in the chilly waters, capture stunning photos, or simply sit on the rocks and marvel at the waterfall’s sheer power and grace. The falls remain largely uncommercialized, preserving their natural charm and inviting travelers to reconnect with nature. Casaroro Falls isn’t just a destination — it’s an experience that highlights the rugged beauty and adventurous spirit of Negros Oriental.

Casaroro Falls

Casaroro Falls is a paradise for adventurers who crave both challenge and beauty. The steep descent, with over 300 concrete steps, tests visitors’ endurance, but the sight of the towering falls at the end of the trail makes every step worthwhile. The rocky riverbed adds an element of excitement, requiring careful footing as you make your way to the base of the cascade. The reward is an unspoiled landscape where the vibrant greens of the forest contrast with the white torrent of water plunging into the pool below. The chilly basin invites swimmers to take a refreshing dip, while the surrounding rocks provide perfect spots for resting and taking in the view. Local guides are available to assist, sharing insights and ensuring a safe yet exhilarating adventure. For those seeking a waterfall experience that combines natural splendor with a touch of rugged exploration, Casaroro Falls is an unmissable gem in the heart of Negros Oriental.
