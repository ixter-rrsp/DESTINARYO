Philippines Tourist Spots

PAGE 10

Subic Bay – Zambales

Once a U.S. naval base, Subic Bay has transformed into a thriving beach destination with luxury resorts, adventure parks, and historical landmarks.

Sumlang Lake- Albay

A serene and picturesque lake in Albay, Sumlang Lake offers a perfect view of Mayon Volcano, along with bamboo rafting, kayaking, and floating cottages.

Taal Volcano – Batangas

One of the world's smallest active volcanoes, located in Batangas. It features a crater lake within a larger lake, creating a unique landscape. Visitors can hike, take a boat ride, or enjoy breathtaking views from Tagaytay’s scenic spots.

Tangadan Falls – La Union

A two-tiered waterfall nestled in the mountains of San Gabriel, La Union, Tangadan Falls is a scenic paradise known for its rock pools, cliff-jumping spots, and serene natural beauty.

Tappiyah Falls – Banaue, Ifugao

Nestled in Banaue, Ifugao, this majestic waterfall boasts a powerful cascade that flows into a deep, emerald pool. Accessible via a scenic yet challenging hike, it offers a rewarding and refreshing experience after exploring the famous rice terraces.

Taytay Falls – Laguna

A hidden gem in Majayjay, Laguna, Taytay Falls (also known as "Imelda Falls") features refreshing ice-cold waters, lush greenery, and a tranquil ambiance perfect for nature lovers.

Tinago Falls – Iligan City, Lanao del Norte

True to its name, Tinago Falls (which means "hidden falls") is a majestic 73-meter-high waterfall tucked within a deep ravine, offering visitors a breathtaking view of cascading waters into a blue lagoon.

Tinuy-an Falls – Surigao del Sur

Often called the "Niagara Falls of the Philippines," Tinuy-an Falls is a stunning 95-meter-wide, multi-tiered waterfall in Bislig, Surigao del Sur, known for its white curtain-like cascades.

Tumalog Falls – Oslob, Cebu

A stunning, veil-like waterfall in Oslob, Cebu, featuring thin, gentle cascades flowing over moss-covered rocks. The shallow pools create a dreamy, relaxing atmosphere, making it a favorite stop after a whale shark-watching adventure in the area.

Zoobic Safari – Zambales

Zoobic Safari, located in Subic Bay Freeport Zone, is the Philippines’ only tiger safari. It offers thrilling encounters with tigers, exotic animals, and indigenous wildlife. Attractions include a petting zoo, serpentarium, Aeta cultural performances, and safari rides through open enclosures.
