Pulangbato Falls

Valencia, Negros Oriental

Pulangbato Falls, named for the striking red-hued rocks that surround it, is a captivating natural wonder tucked away in Valencia, Negros Oriental. The waterfall cascades from a towering cliff, its waters flowing over rust-colored boulders — a unique sight caused by the area's rich mineral deposits. The vibrant contrast between the fiery red rocks and the lush green foliage creates a stunning, almost surreal landscape. The pool at the base of the falls is refreshingly cool, inviting visitors to take a dip and relax in nature's embrace. The area is well-maintained, with picnic huts and pathways that make it easy to explore and fully enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Beyond the falls themselves, the site exudes a tranquil charm, with the sound of rushing water blending with the songs of birds and the rustling of leaves. Pulangbato is part of a hot spring resort, adding another layer of relaxation to your visit. After swimming under the falls, visitors can unwind in naturally heated pools, making for a perfect balance of adventure and rest. The waterfall is easily accessible, with just a short walk from the parking area, making it an ideal spot for families and casual travelers. Whether you’re seeking a picturesque escape or a refreshing retreat, Pulangbato Falls offers a unique and peaceful getaway.

Pulangbato Falls

Pulangbato Falls stands out not only for its beauty but also for its one-of-a-kind red rock formations, giving it a distinct charm among Philippine waterfalls. The falls' name, which translates to "red rock," perfectly captures the site’s dramatic aesthetic, where nature’s artistry is on full display. The nearby hot spring pools provide a soothing contrast to the chilly waterfall, offering visitors a complete nature experience in one location. Locals are friendly and welcoming, often sharing stories about the area’s volcanic origins and the healing properties of the hot springs. The falls are easily accessible, yet the site still feels like a hidden paradise, with fewer crowds compared to other tourist spots. Pulangbato Falls is a must-visit for travelers looking to discover the unique natural wonders of Negros Oriental, where fiery hues meet lush greenery in an unforgettable landscape.
