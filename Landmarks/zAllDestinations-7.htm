Philippines Tourist Spots

PAGE 7

Mount Apo – Davao del Sur

The "King of Philippine Peaks," Mt. Apo is the tallest mountain in the country and a challenging yet rewarding climb for adventure seekers.

Mount Arayat – Pampanga, Central Luzon

A dormant volcano in Pampanga, known for its mythical legends and scenic hiking trails. The summit rewards climbers with panoramic views of Central Luzon, while lush forests provide a habitat for diverse flora and fauna, including rare eagles.

Mount Balagbag- Rizal & Bulacan

A beginner-friendly hiking destination on the Rizal-Bulacan border, known for its open trails and breathtaking sunset views. With its grassy slopes and rock formations, it’s a favorite among trekkers looking for an easy climb near Metro Manila.

Mount Guiting-Guiting – Romblon

Known as one of the most difficult climbs in the Philippines, Mt. Guiting-Guiting is a rugged, knife-edged mountain that attracts experienced mountaineers seeking extreme adventure.

Mount Halcon – Oriental Mindoro

One of the toughest climbs in the Philippines, this Albay peak is known for its challenging trails, dense jungles, and stunning summit views. Rich in biodiversity, it’s home to rare species and offers an unforgettable trekking experience.

Mount Hamiguitan – Davao Oriental

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Mt. Hamiguitan is home to the Philippines' famous pygmy forest, unique plant species, and endemic wildlife like the Philippine Eagle.

Mount Hibok-Hibok – Camiguin

A dormant volcano on Camiguin Island, Mt. Hibok-Hibok is an adventurous climb offering hot springs, lush forests, and a stunning panoramic view of Camiguin and its nearby islands.

Mount Kanlaon- Negros

The tallest peak in the Visayas, Mt. Kanlaon is an active volcano known for its crater lakes, sulfur vents, and challenging treks through dense forests and ridges.

Mount Kitanglad – Bukidnon

A majestic peak in Bukidnon, home to mossy forests, diverse wildlife, and the rare Philippine eagle. Hikers can enjoy a challenging ascent, rewarded with stunning sunrise views and a glimpse of the untouched beauty of Mindanao’s mountains.

Mount Makiling – Laguna

A mystical dormant volcano covered in dense rainforest, Mt. Makiling is known for its myths, rich biodiversity, and challenging yet rewarding hiking trails.
