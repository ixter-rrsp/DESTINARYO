Adventure Sites

PAGE 2

Dahilayan AdventurePark – Bukidnon

A top extreme adventure park in Bukidnon, offering Asia’s longest dual zipline, rope courses, and ATV rides. Its cool mountain setting and adrenaline-pumping activities make it a must-visit for thrill-seekers and nature lovers.

Danao Adventure Park – Bohol

Located in Bohol, this eco-adventure park features heart-pounding activities such as canyon swinging, ziplining, caving, and rappelling. Nestled in a lush landscape, it provides a thrilling outdoor experience for adventure enthusiasts.

Dinosaur Island – Pampanga

A dinosaur-themed park in Pampanga, featuring life-sized, animatronic dinosaurs. Visitors can enjoy interactive exhibits, fossil displays, and a prehistoric jungle tour, making it an exciting attraction for kids and dinosaur lovers.

Enchanted Kingdom – Laguna

The most popular theme park in the Philippines, Enchanted Kingdom offers thrilling rides, magical experiences, and family-friendly entertainment.

Glorious Fantasyland – Zamboanga del Norte

A grand amusement park in Zamboanga del Norte, featuring colorful rides, dazzling light shows, and live entertainment. It is one of Mindanao’s biggest theme parks, providing fun-filled experiences for visitors of all ages.
