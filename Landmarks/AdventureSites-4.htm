Adventure Sites

PAGE 4

People's Park In The Sky – Cavite

A hilltop park in Cavite offering panoramic views of Tagaytay and Taal Volcano. Once an unfinished mansion, it now serves as a scenic viewpoint with cool breezes and a historical charm for visitors.

Sky Ranch – Tagaytay/Pampanga

A popular amusement park with locations in Tagaytay and Pampanga, featuring thrilling rides, a giant Ferris wheel, and breathtaking views. It is a family-friendly destination for fun, entertainment, and relaxation.

Sky Water Park – Cebu

A rooftop water park in Cebu, featuring exciting slides, wave pools, and breathtaking city views. It provides a unique aquatic adventure, making it a top choice for those looking to cool off and have fun.

Star City – Manila

A well-loved amusement park in Manila, featuring exhilarating rides, family-friendly attractions, and live entertainment. It is a top destination for thrill-seekers and families looking for fun and excitement in the city.

Zoobic Safari – Zambales

Zoobic Safari, located in Subic Bay Freeport Zone, is the Philippines’ only tiger safari. It offers thrilling encounters with tigers, exotic animals, and indigenous wildlife. Attractions include a petting zoo, serpentarium, Aeta cultural performances, and safari rides through open enclosures.
