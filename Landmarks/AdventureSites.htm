Adventure Sites

PAGE 1

Anjo World and Snow World – Cebu

The Cebu’s largest theme park, offers thrilling rides, themed attractions, and carnival games. Snow World adds icy slides, sculptures, and a winter wonderland experience. Perfect for families and adventure seekers, the park blends tropical fun with a cool escape, making it one of the top amusement destinations in the Visayas region.

Calauit Safari Park – Palawan

Wildlife sanctuary home to African animals like giraffes and zebras, alongside native Philippine species. Visitors can explore vast landscapes, feed animals, and learn about conservation efforts. The park offers a unique safari experience, bringing the beauty of African wildlife to the tropical setting of Palawan’s scenic Calamian Islands.

Cebu Ocean Park – Cebu

Cebu Ocean Park is the largest oceanarium in the Philippines, offering a diverse marine life experience with massive aquariums, interactive exhibits, and animal encounters. Located in Cebu City, this world-class attraction showcases local and exotic marine species and provides an educational and entertaining experience for visitors of all ages.

Clark Aqua Planet – Pampanga

Clark Aqua Planet is one of the largest water parks in Southeast Asia, covering 10 hectares of thrilling water attractions. Located inside Clark Freeport Zone in Pampanga, this state-of-the-art water park features 38 water slides and attractions, making it a premier destination for families, thrill-seekers, and water lovers.

Clark Safari and Adventure Park – Pampanga

A wildlife and recreational destination featuring over 1,500 animals, thrilling adventure rides, and interactive exhibits. Visitors can enjoy safari tours, animal encounters, and educational experiences in a nature-friendly environment.
