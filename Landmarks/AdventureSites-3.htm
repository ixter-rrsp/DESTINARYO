Adventure Sites

PAGE 3

Inflateable Island – Subic Bay, Zambales

Asia’s largest floating playground, featuring giant slides, swings, and obstacle courses on water. This vibrant attraction offers exciting activities, perfect for thrill-seekers and families looking for a fun beach adventure.

J Castles ThemePark – Laguna

A fairy tale-inspired attraction in Laguna, featuring castle-like structures, themed rides, and magical landscapes. It offers a whimsical experience for families and kids who dream of stepping into a storybook adventure.

Magik Land – Negros Occidental

The first outdoor theme park in Negros Occidental, featuring thrilling rides, carnival attractions, and fantasy-themed entertainment. It is designed for fun and adventure, making it a great destination for families and theme park enthusiasts.

Manila Ocean Park – Manila

A world-class marine theme park in Manila, offering interactive exhibits, ocean tunnels, and fascinating marine shows. Visitors can explore diverse aquatic species, experience fish spa treatments, and even swim with stingrays and sharks.

Ocean Adventure – Subic

A marine park in Subic, featuring live dolphin and sea lion shows, interactive exhibits, and snorkeling experiences. It provides an educational and entertaining experience while promoting marine conservation and wildlife protection.
