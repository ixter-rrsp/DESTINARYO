Zoobic Safari

Zambales

Zoobic Safari is a unique wildlife park nestled within the lush Subic Bay Freeport Zone, offering visitors a one-of-a-kind adventure where they can experience animals up close and personal. Spanning over 25 hectares of natural forest, this zoo and safari park is home to a diverse collection of animals, including tigers, crocodiles, deer, and more. The park’s main attraction, the Tiger Safari, lets visitors ride a caged vehicle as majestic Bengal tigers roam freely, sometimes even hopping onto the vehicle for a thrilling encounter. Other attractions include the Savannah, where guests can see ostriches, wild boars, and camels, as well as the Croco Loco area, featuring a walkway over a crocodile pit. This immersive experience makes Zoobic Safari a must-visit for animal lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Beyond the safari, Zoobic Safari offers interactive and educational experiences for all ages. The Zoobic Park section features a more traditional zoo setting, where visitors can feed and interact with gentle animals like goats and birds. Meanwhile, the Aeta Trail & Show highlights the rich indigenous culture of the Aeta people, with live performances and insights into their traditional hunting techniques. The park is designed to provide both excitement and education, promoting wildlife conservation while giving visitors a chance to connect with nature. Whether you’re watching a tiger up close, feeding baby crocodiles, or learning about native wildlife, Zoobic Safari offers an unforgettable escape into the wild side of Zambales.

Zoobic Safari

Zoobic Safari is a perfect destination for families, friends, and even school field trips, blending thrills, nature, and learning into a single adventure-packed day. The park’s guided tours ensure you don’t miss a thing, while the natural surroundings make for a scenic escape from the city hustle. It’s a place where you can feel the rush of a tiger encounter, marvel at exotic creatures, and gain a deeper appreciation for wildlife all in one visit. Whether you’re seeking a heart-pounding safari ride or a relaxing stroll through animal habitats, Zoobic Safari promises a memorable experience filled with fun, discovery, and plenty of photo-worthy moments.
