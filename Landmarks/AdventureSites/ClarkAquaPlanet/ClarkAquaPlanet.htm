Clark Aqua Planet

Pampanga

Clark Aqua Planet is the largest waterpark in the Philippines, offering a thrilling escape for visitors of all ages with over 38 world-class water attractions spread across a massive 10-hectare space. Located in the heart of Clark Freeport Zone, this water paradise is packed with adrenaline-pumping slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers, making it the perfect destination for a day of fun and relaxation. The Aqua Loop and Super Bowl rides cater to thrill-seekers, while the Flow Rider offers a surfing experience right in the middle of the park. For those seeking a more laid-back vibe, the wave pool and children’s play area provide a refreshing oasis to splash around and soak up the sun.

Beyond the exciting water attractions, Clark Aqua Planet offers top-notch amenities to make your visit as comfortable and convenient as possible. The park features spacious cabanas, food stalls, and lounging areas where guests can unwind and refuel. Safety is a priority, with trained lifeguards stationed throughout the park to ensure everyone can enjoy the rides with peace of mind. Whether you’re sliding down towering tubes, floating along the lazy river, or building sandcastles by the wave pool, every corner of the park is designed to deliver a memorable aquatic adventure for friends, families, and even large groups.

Clark Aqua Planet

Clark Aqua Planet is more than just a waterpark — it’s a destination for fun-filled bonding moments and exciting escapades. It’s a place where you can unleash your inner child, conquer your fears on heart-pounding slides, or simply drift along the currents as you take in the vibrant atmosphere. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or looking for a quick getaway, this water wonderland promises non-stop excitement and hours of aquatic bliss.
