Enchanted Kingdom

Sta. Rosa, Laguna

Step into a world of wonder and excitement at Enchanted Kingdom, the Philippines' first and most iconic theme park, located in Santa Rosa, Laguna. This fantasy-filled destination is perfect for thrill-seekers, families, and friends, offering a wide variety of rides and attractions for all ages. Feel your heart race on the Space Shuttle, a looping roller coaster that takes you through exhilarating twists and turns. For a splash of excitement, hop on the Rio Grande Rapids and brave the roaring waters with your group. Meanwhile, the Grand Carousel and the Flying Fiesta offer whimsical rides that transport visitors to a fairy tale-like realm. With themed zones like Victoria Park, Brooklyn Place, and the Jungle Outpost, every corner of Enchanted Kingdom promises a new adventure to discover.

The magic doesn’t end with the rides — Enchanted Kingdom enchants visitors with spectacular live shows, street performances, and a dazzling fireworks display to cap off the day. Eldar the Wizard, the park’s beloved mascot, adds an extra touch of charm, greeting guests and spreading joy throughout the kingdom. There are plenty of food stalls, souvenir shops, and photo spots to complete the experience, making it easy to spend an entire day exploring and enjoying the park. Whether you’re screaming on the Anchors Away, taking in the view from the Wheel of Fate, or simply soaking in the lively atmosphere, Enchanted Kingdom promises a memorable escape from reality.

Enchanted Kingdom

Enchanted Kingdom is more than just a theme park — it’s a place where cherished memories are made. It’s perfect for celebrations, school trips, or a weekend getaway, offering a blend of thrills, fantasy, and family fun. With its continuous expansions and new attractions, the park always has something fresh and exciting to offer returning visitors.
