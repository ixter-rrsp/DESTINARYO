Manila Ocean Park

Manila

Manila Ocean Park, the first world-class marine theme park in the Philippines, offers an unforgettable underwater adventure in the heart of the city. Home to a stunning array of marine life, the park features a 360-degree ocean tunnel, where visitors can marvel at sharks, rays, and colorful fish swimming overhead. The Oceanarium showcases thousands of aquatic species, giving guests a closer look at the diverse marine biodiversity of the Philippines and beyond. Beyond the tanks, the park offers interactive exhibits like the Sea Lion Show, where these playful creatures charm audiences with their tricks and agility. Manila Ocean Park is a place of wonder and learning, where visitors of all ages can explore the mysteries of the ocean without leaving the city.

But the experience doesn’t end underwater — Manila Ocean Park is packed with unique attractions for a full day of fun. Guests can walk through the Jellies Exhibit, with its mesmerizing display of jellyfish, or visit the Birds of Prey Kingdom to encounter majestic raptors up close. For thrill-seekers, the Trails to Antarctica exhibit features a snow room and a chance to meet friendly penguins. The park also boasts a spa with fish therapy, a mermaid swim experience, and dining areas with views of Manila Bay. Whether you're bonding with family, exploring with friends, or on a solo adventure, Manila Ocean Park offers a perfect blend of entertainment and education for everyone.

Manila Ocean Park

Manila Ocean Park is more than just an aquarium — it's a marine-themed wonderland where visitors can connect with nature in interactive and immersive ways. The park’s diverse attractions make it an ideal destination for school trips, family outings, or even romantic dates by the bay. With its ever-evolving exhibits, live shows, and a chance to see marine creatures up close, the park continues to be a beloved landmark in Manila. Whether you’re watching graceful stingrays glide by, touching starfish, or laughing at sea lion antics, each visit to Manila Ocean Park is a new adventure waiting to unfold.
