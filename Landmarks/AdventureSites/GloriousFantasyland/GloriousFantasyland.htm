Glorious Fantasyland

Dipolog City, Zamboanga Del Norte

Glorious Fantasyland, located in the heart of Dipolog City, Zamboanga del Norte, is a vibrant theme park that brings dreams to life with a mix of thrilling rides, dazzling shows, and enchanting attractions. It stands as the largest theme park in Mindanao, offering a wide range of experiences for visitors of all ages. The roller coasters and drop towers deliver an adrenaline rush, while the carousel and ferris wheel offer a more relaxed, magical vibe. The Haunted House is a must-try for those who crave spine-tingling excitement, with its spooky surprises and twisting pathways. At night, the park transforms into a glowing wonderland, with colorful lights illuminating every corner and adding to the fantasy-like atmosphere.

Aside from the rides, Glorious Fantasyland is known for its spectacular live performances and grand parades that fill the park with energy and music. The Fantasyland Show features talented performers in vibrant costumes, telling magical stories through dance, music, and acrobatics. The park also hosts seasonal events and themed celebrations, keeping the excitement alive all year round. With plenty of food stalls, photo spots, and souvenir shops, visitors can easily spend a whole day immersed in the magic of the park. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, a lover of fantasy, or just looking for a fun day out, Glorious Fantasyland promises a memorable escape full of laughter, excitement, and wonder.

Glorious Fantasyland

Glorious Fantasyland is the perfect destination for families, couples, and friend groups seeking a one-of-a-kind experience in Mindanao. The park’s vibrant energy, diverse attractions, and magical atmosphere make it a top choice for celebrations, vacations, or even just a spontaneous weekend getaway. With its combination of thrilling rides, immersive shows, and picture-perfect scenery, it’s a place where childhood fantasies and grown-up excitement blend seamlessly.
