Beaches

PAGE 3

Kalanggaman Island – Leyte

A picturesque sandbar island in Leyte, famous for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil surroundings. It's a perfect destination for beach camping, snorkeling, and enjoying a pristine tropical escape away from commercialization.

Kaputian Beach – Samal, Davao del Norte

A serene, white-sand beach with sparkling blue waters and a laid-back vibe, Kaputian Beach is a perfect escape for those seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

Long Beach - San Vicente, Palawan

The longest white-sand beach in the Philippines, Long Beach in San Vicente is a tranquil paradise offering an unspoiled escape with breathtaking coastal views.

Magpupungko Rock Pools – Surigao del Norte

A serene stretch of white sand in Bohol, known for its gentle waves, family-friendly resorts, and laid-back atmosphere. Ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and water activities, it's a great alternative to the more crowded Alona Beach.

Nagtabon Beach – Puerto Princesa, Palawan

A hidden gem in Puerto Princesa, Palawan, offering golden sand, rolling waves, and a peaceful, undeveloped coastline. It's an ideal spot for surfing, beachcombing, or simply enjoying the sunset in a serene, crowd-free environment.
